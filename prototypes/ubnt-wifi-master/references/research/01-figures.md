### 1. Andrew von Nagy

- **One-liner**: 把 "capacity-first" 高密度 Wi-Fi 设计框架从模糊直觉变成可计算公式 — 80 人办公室、礼堂、体育场设计的理论奠基人 (evidence: [T01-S007, T01-S008])
- **核心身份**: Principal Architect at Nile (Wi-Fi-as-a-service startup, founded by ex-Cisco/Aruba leadership); ex-Target Senior Wireless Architect (~10 yrs); CCIE-Wireless + CWNE; Tech Field Day delegate (recurring Mobility Field Day)
- **代表作品**:
  - Revolution Wi-Fi blog (2009-present) — capacity planning spreadsheets / channel reuse posts
  - "Design Your WLAN for High Capacity" series (2012-) — quoted by NETGEAR / Cisco / Aruba design white papers
  - MFD (Mobility Field Day) delegate commentary tracks
- **值得读 / 听 / 看的 3 件事**:
  - 📖 *Design Your WLAN for High Capacity* — http://revolutionwifi.blogspot.com/2012/12/design-your-wlan-for-high-capacity_10.html (evidence: [T01-S007])
  - 🎙️ Clear To Send podcast guest episodes (capacity planning + Wi-Fi 7 commentary) — https://www.cleartosend.net/ (evidence: [T01-S033])
  - 🎬 Mobility Field Day MFD14 (May 2026) delegate footage on Tech Field Day YouTube — https://techfieldday.com/event/mfd14/ (evidence: [T01-S008])
- **核心思想关键词** (5):
  - capacity-first design (反义于 coverage-first)
  - airtime budget per client
  - channel reuse math (距离 × dB × overlap threshold)
  - high-density antenna pattern (directional + downtilt)
  - validation > prediction (predictive design ≠ delivered design)
- **voice_samples**:
  - 客户解释样本 (~80 字): 在高密度场景，加 AP 反而让 Wi-Fi 更慢是常见误区 — 真正的瓶颈是同信道干扰（CCI），而不是覆盖。你要做的不是加 AP，是先算每个 AP 的 airtime 余量。 (source: T01-S007 转述自 capacity planning blog 系列)
  - 同业讨论样本 (~50 字): 你给我看 -65 dBm RSSI 没意义，告诉我 client 端的 retry 率和 802.11k 邻居表。 (source: T01-S033 推断自 CTS podcast 多期 capacity 主题; 标推断, 非原话)
  - 标准/专业讨论样本 (~70 字): 802.11ax 的 BSS coloring 在数学上能改善空间复用，但前提是 channel plan 已经合理；如果 channel 重叠严重，BSS coloring 救不了。 (source: T01-S008 转述自 MFD14 delegate commentary)

- **dual_role**: "founder + engineering" (vendor architect + community thought leader)
- **最近 12 个月动态** (since 2025-06):
